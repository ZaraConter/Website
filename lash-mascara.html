Usage Tips

For best results, start with clean, dry lashes. Wiggle the brush at the base of your lashes and sweep upward in a gentle motion. Apply one to two coats for a natural look, or build up for dramatic volume. For added curl, use an eyelash curler before application. To remove, use a gentle eye makeup remover to ensure your lashes stay healthy and conditioned.

Results

Experience immediate volume and length with each application. The innovative formula ensures your lashes look fuller without clumping or flaking throughout the day. Enjoy a beautiful, wide-eyed look that enhances your natural beauty, perfect for both everyday wear and special occasions.

Composition

Our Lash Mascara is enriched with nourishing ingredients, including vitamin E and natural waxes, that condition your lashes while providing stunning definition. The paraben-free formula is designed to be gentle, making it suitable for sensitive eyes and contact lens wearers. The specially designed brush ensures every lash is coated evenly, creating a fanned-out effect that lasts.
